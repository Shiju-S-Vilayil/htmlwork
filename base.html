Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974), known professionally as Vijay, is an Indian actor and singer who works predominantly in Tamil.He is among the highest paid actors in India and has featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list on seven.He has played the lead in 66 films and the International Business Times framed him as a "consistent performer".Referred to as Thalapathy (transl. Commander), Vijay has a significant following .He has won several awards, including an Osaka Best Actor Award and a South Indian International Movie Award.[12][13] In 2023, he became the highest paid actor in India and one of the top paid actors in the world.

Film career

At the age of 10, Vijay started his film career as a child actor in vetri (1984), earning his first paycheck of ₹500 rupees paid for by actor-and-producer P. S. Veerappa. He then performed as a child actor in films such as Kudumbam (1984), Vasantha Raagam (1986), Sattam Oru Vilayaattu (1987) and Ithu Engal Neethi (1988). He also performed in Naan Sigappu Manithan (1985) as a co-star with Rajinikanth, who was the lead actor.

Initially attending Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School[21] at Kodambakkam, Vijay later joined the Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School[22] at Virugambakkam and went on to pursue a degree in Visual Communications from Loyola College. While studying at the age of 18, he asked his father if "he would launch him".
